# Research positioning

CortexMorph-XAI is positioned at the intersection of cortical-surface neuroimaging, geometric deep learning, explainable AI, longitudinal analysis, and agentic scientific reporting.

The project provides evidence of research readiness in:

- cortical-surface terminology and morphometry;
- mesh and graph representation thinking;
- anomaly and longitudinal change analysis;
- interpretable evidence reporting;
- reproducible Python engineering;
- computational neuroscience-oriented communication.
