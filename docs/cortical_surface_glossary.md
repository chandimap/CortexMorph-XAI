# Cortical surface glossary

This project uses simplified synthetic examples, but the terminology mirrors real cortical-surface neuroimaging.

- **Vertex**: a point on a surface mesh.
- **Face**: a triangle connecting three vertices.
- **Pial surface**: the outer cortical boundary in structural MRI processing.
- **White surface**: the boundary between white matter and grey matter.
- **Curvature**: a measure related to local surface bending.
- **Sulcal depth**: a measure related to the depth of folds or sulci.
- **Longitudinal change**: difference in morphometry across timepoints.
- **Surface registration**: aligning cortical surfaces across subjects or timepoints.
